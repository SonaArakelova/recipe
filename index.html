Salmon and Quinoa Bowl

This dish is popular in many cuisines for its balance of protein, healthy fats, and fiber. It`s particularly favored in health-conscious cooking and meal prep due to its versatility and ease of preparation.

Ingredients

For The Fish

  • Salmon fillets (fresh or thawed)
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)
  • Lemon (slices or juice)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, or thyme)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

For The Quinoa

  • Quinoa (1 cup, rinsed)
  • Water or broth (2 cups)
  • Olive oil (1-2 tablespoons)
  • Garlic (minced or powdered, optional)
  • Fresh herbs (like parsley or cilantro, chopped)
  • Vegetables (green been, cucumber, or spinach, chopped)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Quinoa: Rinse it,Bring to a Boil, Simmer and add Vegetables
  2. Cook the Salmon: Rub the fillets with olive oil,lemon juice,salt and pepper,add fresh herbs on top:Place the salmon on a lined baking sheet and bake for about 15 minutes
  3. Place a serving of quinoa on a bowl and top it with a salmon fillet. Garnish with green beans and tomatoes.
  4. Enjoy it!
